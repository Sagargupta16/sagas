# Fanfic check

Quality gate for the fanfic shelf. Fan fiction lives or dies on characterization fidelity and honest labeling.

## Preparation

Read `references/fanfic-conventions.md` from the `writing-craft` library, plus the book's `_bible.md` (canon-facts section, declared divergence point).

## Checks

1. **Canon position declared.** The book.md body or first chapter must state where it sits on the spectrum: canon-compliant, canon-compatible (fills gaps, contradicts nothing), divergent-at-point-X, or AU. If undeclared, propose the declaration; everything below is checked against it.
2. **In-character audit.** For each canon character in the draft:
   - Voice: would this line be recognizable as them with the name covered? Check against their established speech patterns (bible voice notes; for wizarding world, book-canon diction over film memory).
   - Decision test: OOC means contradicting established characterization WITHOUT story justification. Characters may grow past canon behavior, but the growth must be on the page, not assumed.
3. **Canon-fact accuracy.** Names, spells, dates, geography, rules of the source world against the bible's canon-facts section. Distinguish canon from fanon: fanon is usable, but knowingly -- log deliberate fanon in the bible so it's a choice, not an error.
4. **OC balance** (if original characters present): the Mary Sue triad -- does the OC warp canon characters' competence, hijack page-time budgets (no lead below 15%, none above 35%), or get unearned deference from canon characters?
5. **Timeline discipline.** Events keyed to the source timeline: ages, school years, war dates. One-line ledger check against the bible timeline.
6. **Header hygiene.** Non-commercial disclaimer present (the footer covers the site; the book page should still name the universe's rights holders), content warnings for anything in AO3's Big Four territory explicitly warned or explicitly opted out -- never implicit.

## Output

Findings list with severity (blocker / should-fix / note), each citing the convention it trips. Blockers: undeclared canon position, OOC without justification, missing content warning. Publishing waits on blockers; the rest is his call.

## Rules

- Fanon vs canon disagreements: canon wins by default; his deliberate divergence wins over both once declared.
- Never monetization language anywhere near this shelf, including "support links" -- free means free.
